For the good riddance of BAD BUGS

I've grown this Nepenthes Plant for a few years now, and it's gone from a wee little leaf into a full fledged bug eating machine.

It started out hanging in the kitchen, and now it's got it's living the life in it's own tent with it's very own watering system now.

I know there is quite a few pictures in there, but essentially, I hung a tent in my outbuilding. Routed water to it, 3D printed my own funnel to drain to the sink for the excess and have had quite a time going from idea to reality.
